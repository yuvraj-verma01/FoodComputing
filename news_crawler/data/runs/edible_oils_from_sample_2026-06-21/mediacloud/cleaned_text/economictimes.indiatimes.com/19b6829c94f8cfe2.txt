Seized paneer destroyed
The 500 kilograms of paneer were stored at New Garhwal Dairy in Bhangel, Noida, before being destroyed the next day with the support of local authorities. FSSAI’s Deputy Commissioner Sarvesh Kumar said, “This action has been taken to protect public health. During the festival season, the supply of adulterated products increases. Strict monitoring is necessary.”How to check paneer purity
Experts advise checking paneer before buying or consuming it. Fresh paneer should have a mild smell, firm texture, and white colour.
