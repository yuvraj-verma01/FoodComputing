Kolhapur: Sangli police has arrested man (44) from Miraj for printing fake currency notes of Rs50 denomination and bringing the notes into circulation with the help of agents. 
Ahad Mohammad Shaikh, a resident of Shaniwar Peth of Miraj, was printing fake currency notes of Rs50 denomination in a small unit that he set up in his house.
Police got the tip that a man was near Akashwani Kendra with fake notes.
They nabbed him and found the fake currency printing unit. They searched the premises and found fake currency notes worth Rs 1.90 lakh. Sanjay More, inspector of Sangli City Police Station said, âWe have learnt that Ahad Shaikh used to distribute the notes in Sangli, Miraj and Karnataka through agents. We have asked all banks to check currency notes of Rs 50 denomination.â
